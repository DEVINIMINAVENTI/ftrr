Handouts STORE - An online storage that holds access to study materials basically for students at the Federal University of Technology Minna Niger State, Nigeria. Handouts STORE was built by a group of students who saw how important study materials are to the daily struggles regarding themselves and their fellow students, they therefore took it upon themselves to help sort this issue, they therefore concluded on building a website that would hold in it; connections and references to study materials. With Handouts STORE in place,

Students would no longer need to ask their fellow students for handouts and other study materials.

Students get to keep their privacy and respect as they'll henceforth only need to interact with the website made available therefore saving them the trouble of speaking to a third party individual or installing third party apps.

Students can easily and privately search for and download study materials at the comfort of their lodges and homes.

Students are sure to get the best from using our service as we scan, review and ensure all study materials and resources made available on our site are 100% accurate in what they are said to portray and also, free from all harm.

If you check and do not see study materials available for your department, you may contact our support team for support, in the case you're asked to assist our teams with A photo showing the courses needed prolly your Course Reg, you may be nice to assist us so we can update our Servers...

We are also doing our best to make available Past Questions and Tutorial services on our site and media channels we may provide. We want the best for every FUTMITE, we will get there; because success is ours.... ~ Handouts STORE
